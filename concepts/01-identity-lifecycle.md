# Identity Lifecycle Management

The end-to-end process of managing a user's digital identity from creation to deletion.

**Stages:** Provisioning → Active Use → Access Review → Modification → Deprovisioning

**Key controls:**
- Automated provisioning tied to HR system (joiner/mover/leaver process)
- Role-based access assignment at onboarding
- Periodic access reviews (quarterly recommended)
- Immediate deprovisioning on termination

**Exam relevance:** Security+ SY0-701 Domain 4 (Identity & Access), SC-300 Topic 1
